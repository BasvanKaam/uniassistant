Getting access to Nerdio University
NME or NMM customers — self-registration
  1. Go to lfa.getnerdio.com
  2. Enter your Install ID → click Validate
  3. Confirm your company name and domain
  4. Add your main admin contact — becomes the Power User
  5. Add additional users (admins or learners)
  6. Review and Submit

💡 Set up a second Power User as backup. One form per organization — for initial setup only.

Enterprise channel partners
Contact your Nerdio Partner Development Manager (PDM) to gain access. Self-registration is not available for partners.
